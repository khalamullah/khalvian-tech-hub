About Khalvian Tech Hub

We are a technology company dedicated to providing professional SaaS solutions for device and data management.

Khalvian Tech Hub was built to give businesses and individuals a powerful, secure, and easy-to-use platform for managing their connected devices, files, and data in real-time.

Our platform is built with the latest technologies including Django, PostgreSQL, WebSockets, and a modern responsive frontend — ensuring reliability, scalability, and security.
